What are exoplanets?

Interesting Facts About Exoplanets

  • 1RXS J160929.1-210524 b

    The first exoplanet to be photographed.

  • Deadly Rain

    The super-Earth 55 Cancri e, due to its carbon-rich atmosphere, high temperatures, and high pressure, is believed to have diamond rain.

  • Closest Neighbor

    Although Proxima Centauri b is our closest neighbor, it is still 4.24 light-years away.

  • Doppelgänger

    Kepler-452b is so Earth-like that we might get confused, if it weren't for its absurd distance from our Sun!

  • 4546B?!

    Due to its gargantuan proportions, it is likely that somewhere in the universe, there exists a planet similar to the one in Subnautica.

  • Lord of the Rings

    J1407b boasts the largest ring system known to humanity, dwarfing that of Saturn.
